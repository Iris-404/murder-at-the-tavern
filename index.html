Murder at the Tavern

Every murder reveals a magical secret

In a busy tavern filled with magical beings, a brutal murder shakes the establishment, and a sharp-witted detective takes on the challenge of solving the mystery. Each episode focuses on a different suspect, each hiding secrets and lies. As the detective interrogates them, he uncovers a web of deception, hidden motives, and ancient grudges. Tensions rise with every revelation as listeners join the detective in piecing together the truth.
